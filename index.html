About Zubuyer Kaolin

Zubuyer is a film maker, animator, inventor and an award winning astrophotographer who is on a journey to tell stories through different medium. He is constantly finding new ways to converge his love of art, technology and photography by pushing the boundaries of what's possible. His work has garnered attention from the likes of European Space Agency, Sony, Adobe and many more. His work is enjoyed by millions around the world and inspired many to follow in his footsteps. His goal is to keep expanding his horizon of creativity and find new ways to tell great stories.
